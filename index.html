Clearing Guide

Two jobs per booking: pre-qualify it (gather info for the rep) or disqualify & cancel it (free the slot). When unsure, leave it.

✅ Who to pre-qualify

  • Hasn't had a real conversation with a sales rep (automated messages don't count)
  • Hasn't been asked any pre-qualifying questions (asking for their website doesn't count)

⛔ Who to disqualify

  • Business is on the DQ list (see below)
  • "Disqualified / DQ" shows in the yellow internal comments or in the dispositions

When in doubt, leave the appointment — it's safer than cancelling.

📨 SMS or email? ▸

💬 Lean SMS

  • They've replied to a text before
  • Email is set to Do-Not-Disturb
  • There's already real SMS back-and-forth

✉️ Lean Email

  • Ignored a string of texts & calls
  • Opened a prior email
  • Wrong number / call-center number
  • Lots of voicemails already left
🧭 How to clear a calendar ▸
  1. Work day by day, every closer — finish Monday across all, then Tuesday, etc.
  2. Open each booking → its contact.
  3. Check in order: Disqualified? → business fit? → yellow notes? → already pre-qualified?
  4. Pre-qualify new bookings, or DQ & cancel the clear ones.
  5. Delete crossed-out (cancelled) appointments to clean the view.
  6. Log cancellations & anything you're unsure about; record your time.
🧭
Only cancel on a clear DQ/Disqualified flag or a budget-dequeue note. Any hesitation → leave it.
